What's Pizza Builder

Pizza Builder is an open source web application for your online Pizza Store. It allows your customers to create new pizzas, modify existing ones, save their favorites flavors (including custom flavors) and buy the pizzas based on their own taste and preferences. You have total control on your menu options, available flavors and ingredients, user customization limitations, and more.
